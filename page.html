About

I'm so glad you found your way to my website! My name is Kelli Olsen, and I am a birth doula serving Idaho Falls, Idaho and the surrounding areas. I have always had a passion for supporting and empowering women and families, and I feel privileged to be able to offer my services to those who are welcoming a new life into the world. I am happily married to my wonderful husband since 2022 and together we have enjoyed building our life together. Although I don't have children of my own yet, I have been blessed to witness the joy and beauty of birth firsthand. I'm so excited to be on this journey with you to help ensure that your birth and postpartum experience is as positive and empowering as possible.

Birth Doula :

a person professionally trained to provide support to the client and their partner(s) during labor and birth.

The Birth Package:

My Birth Package includes comprehensive support to ensure a positive and empowering birth experience. You'll receive two prenatal visits where we'll get to know each other and discuss your birth preferences. I'll be with you throughout your labor and birth, providing guidance and emotional support every step of the way. Afterward, I'll visit you for one postpartum visit to offer additional assistance and answer any questions you may have. You can contact me anytime during your pregnancy via text and phone calls, and I'll be on call for you 24/7 starting two weeks before your due date. *Please note that this package is flexible and can be tailored to meet your specific needs. I am here to support you in whatever way you need throughout your pregnancy, birth, and postpartum journey.
